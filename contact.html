How can we help you?

RWBC Zero Waste stands for Resuable Water Bottles & Container Zero Waste.

Reusable water bottles reduce the amount of both plastic production and waste. Unlike single-use plastic bottles, reusable water bottles are less likely to end up in a landfill. They lower the amount of waste in landfills, reducing pollution.

We buy different types of PET wastes such as used PET bottles, PET Flakes / Granules, PET Chips, PET Lumps / Purge, Yarn Waste, PET Sheet Waste, Pet Strap Regrind and other similar wastes.

We sell different types of PET wastes such as used Recycled Polyester Staple Fibre(RPSF), High Tenacity Recycled Polyester Staple Fibre(HTRPSF), Recycled PET flakes.
